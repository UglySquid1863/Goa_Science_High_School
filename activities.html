Our Vibrant Activities

At Goa Science High School, we balance academics with extracurriculars that build teamwork, discipline, and joy. From parades to performances, these experiences shape well-rounded leaders. Flip cards for details—click images to enlarge!

CTP Military Parade

Our students in crisp uniforms shine in competitions, showcasing discipline and unity. A true display of school pride!

Majorettes Performance

Led by our smiling captain, the team twirls batons in perfect sync—energy and grace that lights up every event.

Street Dance Extravaganza

Centered on our star in yellow, the crew brings rhythm and flair—celebrating creativity and teamwork on stage.

MAPEH Girls Unit March

Stepping in unison, our girls embody poise and coordination in health and arts programs. Inspiring stuff!

DRRM Awareness March

Leading with flags high, our team marches for disaster resilience—forward-thinking action with community at heart.

Elementary Outreach

Partnering with young learners, our students lead fun sessions—building bonds and sharing knowledge casually yet impactfully.

Foundation Anniversary Splash

Students wave joyfully under water sprays—celebrating our school's legacy with pure energy and unity!
