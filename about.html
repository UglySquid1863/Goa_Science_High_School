Get to Know Us

Goa Science High School is an outstanding secondary school offering education from grades 7 to 12 with a focus on science, technology, and mathematics. Our mission is to provide balanced skills for economic productivity, sustainable subsistence, societal contribution, and personal well-being.

“Educating the mind without educating the heart is no education at all.” – Aristotle

Aristotle emphasizes holistic education, aligning with our values.

Our Values

  • Community Impact
  • Personal Growth
